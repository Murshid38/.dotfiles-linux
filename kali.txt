linux headers -> C header files for kernel, important to compile external kernel modules to compile them. like NVidia/AMD GPU drivers, VBox guest additions, wifi or proprietary drivers. and also helpful for building kernel aware apps and for debugging.

vbox guest additions - additional drivers and applications to improve performance and integration of guest os.

GPU drivers - A GPU driver is a piece of software that allows your operating system to communicate with your graphics card (GPU).
The driver exposes APIs like OpenGL, Vulkan, DirectX to your system and apps.

two nvidia gpu drivers - nvidia(proprieraty), nouveau(community, open source, limited accelration, no cuda)
